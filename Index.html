Visual & Technical Elements

üìä
Slide Usage

  • Minimalist: One idea per slide
  • Visual: More images, less text
  • Support: Complement, don't compete with speaker
  • Optional: Many successful talks use no slides

üé®
Design Principles

  • Typography: Large, readable fonts (min. 24pt)
  • Colors: Limited palette, high contrast
  • Simplicity: 6x6 rule (max 6 lines, 6 words)
  • Consistency: Uniform visual style throughout

üé¨
Production

  • Stage: Iconic TED red circle
  • Lighting: Professional, warm setup
  • Cameras: Multiple angles for dynamic viewing
  • Audio: High-quality wireless microphones
